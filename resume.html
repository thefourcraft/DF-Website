Welcome To
My World


Welcome to my personal website,
where I invite you to delve into the
multifaceted world of David Furman.
My career is an exhilarating blend of roles that
keeps me engaged around the clock,
tackling challenges with unbridled enthusiasm.

By day, I am a devoted DevOps Engineer,
serving with dedication at the prestigious
Israel Defense Forces (IDF).
Within these classified walls,
I work relentlessly to optimize and streamline
complex systems, ensuring their
readiness for any mission.

But as the sun sets and the evening unfolds,
a transformation occurs.
I assume the pivotal role of CISO at AiDock Ltd.,
where my mission is crystal clear:
safeguarding digital assets
and orchestrating impeccable cybersecurity strategies.
In this realm, I navigate the ever-evolving landscape of
threats with a vigilant eye and an unwavering commitment
to the security of our digital realm.

And then, as night falls, my persona takes
yet another captivating turn.
I proudly wear the mantle of CTO at IMS - Network,
Israel's most advanced gaming community.
Here, I am in my element, marrying my profound love for
technology with my passion for gaming.
Guiding a dedicated team, I lead the charge in crafting
an extraordinary gaming experience that
sets new standards of excellence.

But even in the midst of this whirlwind,
I manage to carve out precious moments of free time.
In these moments, I find myself contributing
to open-source projects and nurturing my own brand,
"DF," a testament to my enduring dedication
to the world of technology.

Through every twist and turn of my journey,
my unwavering passion for
technology and gaming remain the driving
force that fuels my relentless pursuit of excellence.
Each new challenge is an opportunity embraced
with open arms, regardless of the hour.
My story is a testament to the fusion of
technology and gaming, a story that continues
to evolve, and I invite you to join me on this
captivating adventure.
